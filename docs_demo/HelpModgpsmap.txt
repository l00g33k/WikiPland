* Format of .map file:
    16      # line 1: x of top left of graphic map
    8       # line 2: y of top left of graphic map
    -180    # line 3: longitude of top left of map
    90      # line 4: latitude of top left of map
    1983    # line 5: x of bottom right of graphic map
    991     # line 6: y of bottom right of graphic map
    180     # line 7: longitude of bottom right of map
    -90     # line 8: latitude of bottom right of map
